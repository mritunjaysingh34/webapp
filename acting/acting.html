Dimpy Mishra

Acting Coach

Dimpy Mishra is a theatre stalwart, dedicating a substantial portion of his career to the stage. Well-versed with the traditional and modern acting theories, Dimpy Mishra is a teacher whose communication with students is a thing of class. He has offered his talents in films like ‘Bhoomi’, ‘Matto ki Cycle’ and the web series’ Ashram.’

Career Prospects

After pursuing the course in acting, there are splendid career opportunities on the horizon. You can join a theatre troupe and perform in various plays to become a master of the stagecraft. You can also pursue your interest in the field of cinema, where you can present yourself as a character actor and slowly make your way to the top. Apart from the mainstream roles in acting, you can also choose to become a personality trainer, drama therapist, voice-over artist and arts administrator in corporates and other big firms.
